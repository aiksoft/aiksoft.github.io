About Us

Aikien Softwares Incorporated (a.k.a. AikSoft Inc.) is a software company based in Yenagoa, Bayelsa State, Nigeria. It was established in January, 2021 by Mr. Abasi Aikien, a graduate of Mechatronic Engineering from UCSI University, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, with brilliant academic track record and a few years of experience in programming.

The company's core values are Integrity, Creativity, socio-economic development and Humanitarianism among others. The company is a fast growing company.

At AikSoft our goal is to make your everyday task, wether domestic, corporate or commercial, much easier for you, our highly esteemed customer, by developing efficient, robust and reliable computer programs and handy utility tools that helps you to perform your manual task in an efficient manner, whether at home, office, business, etc. At AikSoft we know the awesome power of using tailor-made software in carrying out your required task in a more efficient manner. This is why the company's moto simply states "...easier with software". This is our goal, to make life much easier and stress-free for you.
